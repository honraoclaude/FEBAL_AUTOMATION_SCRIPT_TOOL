<objective>
Build the PURE deterministic self-healing scorer — the keyless, stdlib-only core that every other Phase-8 plan consumes. Four [0,1] similarity sub-scores (DOM-structure Jaccard, bounding-box IoU visual, a11y role+name difflib, historical-chain match) blend through frozen tunable weights into a [0,1] confidence; the confidence + a live-match count resolve to one of three outcomes through a HARD uniqueness gate applied BEFORE the bands. No LLM, no browser, no I/O — table-testable on fixture dicts and (in plan 02) byte-vendorable into the in-spec layer.

Purpose: HEAL-01 (the 4-strategy blend + priority chain) and HEAL-02 (the 3-outcome banding + never-weaken-assertions invariant) live here as pure logic. This is the single closest sibling of kg/risk.py — clone its discipline exactly.
Output: app/services/healing/{__init__,confidence,geometry,candidates}.py + heal threshold settings + four unit test files (Wave-0 scaffolds for the whole phase).
</objective>
